### System Log Entry Delay Change

To improve the performance of our system log, queries to the `/logs` endpoint that include an `until` parameter may now return results that are delayed up to 10 seconds.

### Bug Fixed in 2018.20

* Group search queries would sometimes incorrectly treat underscores as wildcard characters. (OKTA-164390)
